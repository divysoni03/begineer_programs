Avengers

Iron man



Iron Man is a fictional superhero who wears a suit of armor. His alter ego is Anthony Edward ''Tony'' Stark. He was created by Stan Lee, Jack Kirby and Larry Lieber for Marvel Comics in Tales of Suspense #39 in the year 1963 and appears in their comic books.


Captain America



Captain America is a fictional superhero from the Marvel Comics. He made his first appearance in the comic book Captain America Comics #1 in 1941. He wears a suit that has a white star on his chest with 2 stripes (still white) on each side next to the star. His suit is blue.


Hulk


The Incredible Hulk's alter ego is nuclear scientist Robert Bruce Banner, inventor of the gamma bomb, who absorbed a vast quantity of gamma rays after risking his life to save a teenager. The Incredible Hulk embodies the darkest, angriest, and most antisocial aspects of Banner's personality.


Thor


Thor's name was the Germanic word for thunder. He was a warrior who had enormous strength. In pictures he was shown as a large man with a red beard. He always had with him a magic hammer called Mjollnir.


Black widow


Black Widow has been enhanced by biotechnology that makes her body resistant to aging and disease and she heals faster than the average human.
